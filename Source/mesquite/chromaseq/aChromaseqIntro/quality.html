Quality Scores

Mesquite can color cells based on quality from Phred/Phrap. Each called base is assigned a score, reflecting the error probability of the call. Quality coloring can be toggled on/off by touching on the button below the taxon names or from the Matrix menu: Matrix > Color Cells > Quality from Phred/Phrap. Higher quality base calls are paler (white), while low quality calls are darker (blue). Sites with ambiguous base calls are colored grey.

An example of quality-colored cells. The first sequence has high quality base calls for all characters shown. The second sequence has multiple low-quality calls and two ambiguous sites (Y = C or T, R = A or G).

In addition to coloring the cells, Mesquite colors the background of the taxon name based on a composite quality score for the entire sequence. Higher qualilty sequences are paler, while lower quality sequences are darker.
